# Labby

Labby is a unified control app for managing multiple services from a single interface on iOS. Instead of juggling different apps or web interfaces, Labby lets you log in to your favorite services and control them all in one place.

## Features

- **Multi-Service Support:** Connect and control multiple services from a single app.
- **Unified Login:** Authenticate with each service directly within Labby.
- **Service Control:** Manage, monitor, and interact with your services without switching apps.
- **Expandable Architecture:** Easily add new services with minimal effort.

## Supported Services

- **Proxmox:** Manage your virtual machines and containers.
- **QBittorrent:** Control your torrent downloads and settings.

## Getting Started (User)

1. Download the iPA from the Register.
2. Sideload the iPA on your iOS device using an app such as [Sideloadly](https://sideloadly.io)
3. Launch Labby and log in to your desired services.

## Getting Started (Developer)

1. Clone the repository.
2. Open the project in Xcode.
3. Build and run Labby on your iOS device or simulator.
