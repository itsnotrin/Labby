## General Questions

### What is Labby?

Labby is a unified control app for managing multiple services from a single interface on iOS. Instead of juggling different apps or web interfaces, Labby lets you log in to your favorite services and control them all in one place.

### Which services does Labby support?

Currently, Labby supports:
- Proxmox
- QBittorrent

More services will be added in future updates.

### Is Labby available on platforms other than iOS?

Currently, Labby is only available for iOS devices. Support for additional platforms may be considered in the future.
